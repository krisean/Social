## Developer Roles (Detailed)

### Kris – Technical Visionary
- **Ownership:** Turborepo monorepo, Supabase schema, realtime architecture
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Oversee engine scalability and multi-game architecture
  - Drive OpenAI moderation integration
  - Lead 40% sales strategy (co-founder relationships, venue partnerships)
- **Commission:** $100/month per venue closed (3-month window)

### Pat – Hybrid Growth Engineer
- **Ownership:** Game feature delivery, trial-to-paid conversion, growth metrics
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Build and ship Top Comment MVP (Weeks 1–4)
  - Lead VIBox integration and venue dashboard (Weeks 5–8)
  - 40% business time: 10 venue pilots, conversion tracking
- **Commission:** $100/month per venue closed (3-month window)

### Eric – Product/UX Lead
- **Ownership:** All user-facing experiences and PWAs
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Design and build Top Comment player interface
  - Build VIBox queue display and venue TV UX
  - Create venue staff dashboard (scan counts, revenue, trial status)
  - Internal admin panel (QR generation, venue management)
- **Tech Stack:** React/TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, mobile-first PWA design

### Braden – Infrastructure Engineer
- **Ownership:** Supabase, deployment, observability
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Schema design (venues, games, rounds, analytics)
  - Realtime subscriptions and RLS policies
  - Vercel deployment and uptime monitoring
  - Cost optimization (Supabase billing, third-party APIs)
- **Part-time (20 hrs):** Focus on stability and scaling foundations
